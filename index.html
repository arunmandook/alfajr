About Us

Dedicated to Your Recovery Journey

Al Fajr Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation Centre has been a trusted cornerstone of healthcare in Ras Al Khaimah. Located in Al Safa Building, Al Nakheel, we provide evidence-based physiotherapy and rehabilitation services to patients of all ages.

Our licensed specialists combine the latest clinical techniques with personalised care, helping you regain function, reduce pain and restore your quality of life.

Shoulder Pain & Dislocation
